THE RECIPES
BERRY SCARY PROTEIN SHAKE
Ingredients: 1 serving of vanilla protein powder (Try dotFit's Whey Smooth Vanilla Crème!)
¼ cup of blueberries
¼ cup of strawberries
½ a banana
1 cup of spinach
4 oz of ice
8 oz of milk of choice



ORANGE SCREAMSICLE PROTEIN SHAKE
Ingredients:
1 serving of vanilla protein powder
½ an orange
½ of a banana (Tip: use a frozen banana. Peel and slice it before freezing.)
½ cup of riced cauliflower
8 oz of milk of choice
2 drops of liquid stevia *optional



GREEN GOBLIN PROTEIN SHAKE
Ingredients:
1 serving of protein powder (Go plant based with dotFit's Plant Protein!)
½ of a green apple
½ of a banana (Tip: use a frozen banana. Peel and slice it before freezing.)
¼ of an avocado
1 cup spinach
1 tbsp chia seeds
8 oz of milk of choice



MONSTER MATCHA PROTEIN SHAKE
Ingredients:
1 tsp of matcha
1 serving of vanilla protein powder
½ a banana
½ cup of mango
4 oz of ice
8 oz of milk of choice
Short on time? Try this simple recipe by throwing all ingredients together in your UFC GYM Blender Bottle, shake, and go!



NUTTY COUNT CHOCULA SHAKE

Ingredients:
8 oz of milk of choice (Tip: sub 4 oz with water to reduce calories)
1 scoop of chocolate protein powder
1 tbsp powdered peanut butter (omit if you are sensitive or allergic)
½ tsp of cinnamon
4 oz of ice
Shake & Go!
